Assignment 3

This assignment required the creation of a choropleth map. I used data about California school districts to create a map showing the population density of students in each district.

Assignment 4

This assignment required the creation of a graduated symbol map. I used data about nuclear reactors around the world, and show the number of people within 300 miles of the plant who would be affected by radiation in the event of an exposure.

Assignment 5

This assignment required the creation of a bivariate symbol map. I used the same data as in Assignment 3, showing student population density in California. The map additionally shows the proportion of white vs non-white students in a school district.

Assignment 6

This assignment required the creation of a flow map. My map shows the inflow and outflow of migrants between states from 1887 to 1924. The states show the total amount of migrant both in and out of the state, and the flows show the number of migrants and direction between two states.
